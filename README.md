# Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

 ## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using the SampleSuperstore.csv dataset from Kaggle.

The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations. To analyze and visualize sales performance dashboard based on -
1. Region, Category, Segment
2. Profitability
3. Discounts Impact
4. Shipping Trends.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- West region generated the highest revenue.
- Technology category contributed the highest profit.
- The business generated 2.30M in total sales with an overall profit of 286.40K.
- A total of 38K products were sold across different categories.
- Consumer customers contributed approximately 51% of total sales.
- Phones generated the highest sales among all sub-categories.
- Most customers selected Standard Class shipping.
  
---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase Sales in Low-Performing Regions
- Focus on High-Profit Categories
- Improve Furniture Profitability
- Develop targeted campaigns to increase purchases from Corporate and Home Office segments.
- Promote Underperforming Products
